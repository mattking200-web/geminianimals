B1.1: Animals Changing Our World

Did you know animals are busy workers? Sometimes, their hard work makes life tricky for humans. This is called an impact.

The Neighborhood Bandits (Raccoons)

Raccoons love to eat! In the city, they have learned that human trash cans are like a buffet. They knock them over and leave a big mess for us to clean up!

Nature Hero Tip: We shouldn't be mad at raccoons. They are just trying to find lunch!

B1.2: Humans Helping Habitats

Humans build roads, houses, and factories. Sometimes, this accidentally hurts animal homes (habitats). But we can be Problem Solvers!

Wildlife Bridges

When we build a big road through a forest, animals like deer or bears can't get to the other side to find food or their families. Scientists now build Green Bridges covered in trees so animals can cross safely above the cars!

Nature Hero Tip: Making a small change, like planting a pollinator garden, helps bees and butterflies find a new home in your backyard!
